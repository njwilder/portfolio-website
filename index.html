About Me

One of my greatest joys in life is solving technical problems. A former librarian and Datacenter lab technician for the University of New Hampshire's 'InterOperability Laboratory', I excel in a strong teamwork oriented programming/debugging environment. I enjoy digging into complex issues and collaborating with others (or working solo utilizing the 'rubber ducky' technique) to get the job done, and done right™.
